About D'Antoine (D)

D’Antoine Newsome is a native of Clarksville, Tennessee and is currently in pursuit of of his Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from Belmont University. His journey as a marching musician began in 2013 at Kenwood High School where he played bass drum, snare drum, and served as the drumline captain as well as the assistant field commander in 2016. During the winters of 2015-2017, D’Antoine also competed in Winter Guard International as a bass drummer with Audio Theater. Most recently, he completed his second season with the 8-time world champion percussion ensemble, Music City Mystique, earning the bronze medal at the 2019 Winter Guard International World Championships.
